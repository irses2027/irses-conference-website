Conference Scope

IRSES 2027 invites work on intelligent energy technologies, resilient grids, cyber-physical infrastructure, sustainable systems, efficient hardware, and research connecting policy, markets, and climate-aware engineering practice.

Call for Papers

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers aligned with the conference themes. Manuscripts must follow the IEEE conference format and will be reviewed by the Technical Program Committee.

Publication

Accepted and presented papers will be considered for submission to IEEE Xplore after the conference, subject to IEEE requirements and conference quality checks.

  • At least one author must complete registration for each accepted paper.
  • Camera-ready papers must satisfy the required IEEE PDF compliance checks.
  • Publication is subject to peer review, presentation, copyright, and post-conference screening.
